Air Quality Engineering and Climate Research Group

Lead by Dr. Joshua Fu, the ACS Group is based in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. Our research focus on climate change modeling and the impacts of extreme events. Specifically, we work on air quality impact assessments, transportation planning and energy usage, land use change, emissions tracking, and energy optimization planning.
